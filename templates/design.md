# Design

## Goal

Describe the change you want to make in one or two sentences.

## Constraints

- List hard limits.
- List compatibility or rollout concerns.

## Options

- Option 1: minimal change.
- Option 2: broader refactor.
- Option 3: staged rollout.

## Decision

State the chosen approach and why it fits the project.

## Plan

1. Make the smallest safe change.
2. Add or update tests.
3. Verify the behavior.

## Risks

- Note the main risks and how they will be checked.
